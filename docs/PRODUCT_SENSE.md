# Product Sense

This file frames who the project serves and why it exists. Keep it brief.

## Who this is for

Anyone with a collection of documents — research papers, manuals, contracts, reports — who needs to find specific information without reading through everything manually.

## Core value

- Ask questions in plain language, get precise answers with references to exact pages.
- No manual tagging or keyword guessing. Upload files and start searching.
- Works at any scale, from a handful of documents to thousands.
